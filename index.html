Who am I?

Dakuma is a music producer whose longtime dream of making music has become a reality. Rediscovering his passion during the COVID-19 pandemic, Dakuma has dedicated the past year to producing tracks and has found immense joy in the process. Specializing in Tech House, Trance, Downtempo, Lofi, and Folk, Dakuma's compositions showcase his diverse musical influences and his ability to evoke emotions through captivating soundscapes.

Driven by a deep commitment to craftsmanship, Dakuma aspires to produce tracks for other artists while building a catalog of music he can be proud of. With each release, he aims to leave a lasting impact on the music industry, sharing his transformative creations with the world.

Dakuma's journey is a testament to the belief that it's never too late to pursue your dreams. He emphasizes the limitless nature of artistic expression and the importance of dedication and unwavering love for the craft. Join Dakuma as he shapes his musical destiny, infuses Folk music with his personal touch, and adds his unique voice to the electronic music scene.
